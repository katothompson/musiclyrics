This is an example of a scatterplot created using D3js. The sentiment analysis was done using Sentiment by diy, and the Billboard Top 100 songs, lyrics and ranks were collected by walkerkq and can be found here.

The radius of each circle is scaled to the rank of the song it represents. The more popular the song, the larger the radius. The slider is used to filter the songs by rank. Songs ranking higher than the slider value are displayed. Hovering over a circle causes a tootip to appear with information about the song.
